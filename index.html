About

Wholeness Mami is an ecommerce website that deals in selling wellbeing products like supplements, whole fruits and vegetables, natural soaps and body creams etc. The major goal of creating this website was to provide affordable but healthy fruits and vegetables, supplements, etc.

This is because most of the wellbeing sites available today, are highly expensive that only the rich can afford to purchase these products. This leaves a big number of elderly, middle aged women and their child un able to access these products. The major focus for Wholeness Mami is to achieve affordability with great quality products.
